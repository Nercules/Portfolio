My Internship Journey

Exploring AI in Healthcare Research

Welcome to my website, where I share my exciting internship journey with you. Over the past few months, I had the incredible opportunity to work at the AI Medical Sensing and Diagnostic Laboratory, located at the Holon Institute of Technology (HIT) in Holon, Israel.

During my internship, I got to explore the fascinating world of AI-driven medical research. It's where advanced technology meets the needs of the healthcare industry. I worked on various projects and collaborated with talented researchers and professionals, which helped me grow and learn.

One of the main focuses of my internship was using data analysis techniques to gain valuable insights in healthcare. With machine learning algorithms, I analyzed data to improve medical diagnostics and decision-making.

I worked with complex datasets, fine-tuned predictive models, and evaluated their performance. From preparing the data to choosing the best models, I had to be creative, precise, and pay attention to details.

Besides the technical aspects, my internship provided opportunities for personal and professional growth. I had valuable discussions, learned from world leading experts, and connected with industry professionals.
