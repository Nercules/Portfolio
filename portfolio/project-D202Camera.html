Person Counter

Introduction

Teachers of the Digital Innovation (DI) program would like to know how many of students are in their classroom every day. Sense you are allowed to work hybrid in this program, some students work from home. Therefor the number of students in class can vary.

Solution

Place the camera in the corner of the room filming the entire room. Livestream the camera's footage to a software that would count the total number of people in the room. These results would be kept on a server for further use.
